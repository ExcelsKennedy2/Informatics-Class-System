Human Computer Interaction

Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) focuses on designing and evaluating user-friendly systems that enhance how people interact with technology. The course covers usability principles, user interface design, and the psychology of human interaction with computers. Students will learn how to create intuitive, accessible, and engaging interfaces by applying design techniques and user-centered approaches.
